## Kindergarten Management System

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/5353636966714acb8f178c39b25f10c2/ghb20250919) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript`, etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (Any one of the listed software can be used to run)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Feature Description
The system is divided into (roles): regular users (parents), teachers, administrators.

**Regular User Features:**
1. Login
2. Registration
3. Personal Center
4. Change Password
5. Child Information
6. Payment Information
7. Child Leave Request
8. Child Health Check
9. Menu Information



**Teacher Features:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center - Change Password
3. Personal Center - Personal Information
4. Child Information Management
5. Class Information Management
6. My Work Log
7. Meeting Record Management
8. Todo Item Management
9. Staff Assessment Management
10. Leave Information Management
11. Payment Information Management
12. Child Leave Management
13. Child Health Check Management
14. Resource Information
15. Ingredient Information
16. Menu Information


**Administrator Features:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center
3. User Management
4. Teacher Management
5. Child Information Management
6. Class Information Management
7. Work Log Management
8. Meeting Record Management
9. Todo Item Management
10. Staff Assessment Management
11. Leave Information Management
12. Payment Information Management
13. Child Leave Management
14. Child Health Check Management
15. Resource Information Management
16. Ingredient Information Management
17. Menu Information Management
